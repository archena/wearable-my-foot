WHY:

  Are you interested in monitoring your gait and posture?
  Do you have recurring injuries that are hard to diagnose?
  Do you want some more insight into what your feet are doing?
  Have you always wanted to duct tape electrical components to your trainers?

  
HOW:

  Build your own wearable IoT device for your shoes. Wearable my foot is an
  open source project that shows you how you can do it with readily available hardware.

  * Buy the bits - we provide a shopping list of what you need.
  * Put the bits together - following our instructions.
  * Attach it to your shoe(s) - using gratuitous amounts of duct tape.
  * Go for a walk / jog / disco shuffle - collect some data.
  * Deploy the software to visualise the data and see what your feet are really up to. 


WHAT:

  * Hardware list
  * Microcontroller code
  * Android App
  * Data dashboard
